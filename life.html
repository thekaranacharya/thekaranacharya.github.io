Life

You're not your job. You're not how much money you have in the bank.
You're not the car you drive. You're not the contents of your wallet.
You're not your f*king khakis. You're the all-singing, all-dancing crap of the world.

- Tyler Durden, Fight Club (1999)

Everything is made up of strings, even us mortal humans, according to String Theory.
We're like balloons. All balloons have the same constituent inside : air.
So how do we tell one from the other?
They just come in different sizes and colors.
It's exactly like that with us.

We try to advertise (sell) ourselves on our social media platforms, making the best quips and posting the wittiest comments.
We try to be unique. We gamble. For attention & validation.
But aren't we forgetting we're all basically just a beautiful, unique collection of interlinked cells (and strings within) trying to live on oxygen?

The needs of a happy life are few. The best things in life are free.

There's more to life than work.


My Leisure Interests

Films & TV


Love getting lost in the world of Films & TV shows! Like wormholes, they also transport us to different dimensions of "mind-space" and time.
As a fellow cinephile, I can talk all day about this topic.
Interstellar, The Social Network, Arrival & The Dark Knight are few of my favorite movies of all time.

Books


As one of my favorite (analog) leisure activities, I prefer paperbacks over e-books on a long weekend afternoon with a cup of tea. Stirred, not shaken.
Deep Work, Everything is F*ked: A Book about Hope and Outliers are some of the best books I've read until now.

Music
