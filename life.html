Life

You're not your job. You're not how much money you have in the bank.
You're not the car you drive. You're not the contents of your wallet.
You're not your f*king khakis. You're the all-singing, all-dancing crap of the world.

- Tyler Durden, Fight Club (1999)

Everything is made up of strings, even us mortal humans, according to String Theory. We're like balloons.
All balloons have the same constituent inside : air. So how do we tell one from the other? They just come in different sizes and colors.
It's exactly like that with us.
We try to advertise (sell) ourselves on our social media platforms, making the best quips and posting the wittiest comments.
We try to be unique. We gamble. For attention & validation.
But aren't we forgetting we're all basically just a beautiful, unique collection of interlinked cells (and strings within) trying to live on oxygen?

The needs of a happy life are few. The best things in life are free.

There's more to life than work.

Malcolm Gladwell, Cal Newport, Mark Manson & Jordan B. Peterson are some of the authors whose work I appreciate.

Music

"Music gives a soul to the universe, wings to the mind, flight to the imagination and life to everything."
